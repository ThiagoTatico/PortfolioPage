My name is Thiago Tatico, I had my first contact with programming in Computer Science college, but I ended up staying away from the area for a while. However, in August 2021 my interest in programming and technology so returned. One of the main factors that encouraged me to want to pursue a career in programming was everything I learned in the CS50x course at Harvard, which shows everything from the most basic programming fundamentals to current and extremely relevant concepts, with an excellent balance between practice and theory. The course is one of the best ways to introduce someone to the world of programming and motivate you to move forward, covering programming languages such as: C, Python and JavaScript, as well as HTML and CSS.
